North and Central Kolkata Nearest Durga Puja Pandal guide with Map ::


First destination is the Sealdah Railway Athletic Club. You can visit this place from anywhere in Kolkata. While here, you will automatically reach the College Square Durgotsab Committee because of the barricades. Now moved to Md. Ali Park and after, go to Santosh Mitra Square. It’s a set of puja pandals, and if you plan to visit at night, it’s enough for that night. After, take a bus for Khanna stoppage and go for Nalin Sarkar Street Sarbojanin Durgotsab, Hatibagan Sarbojanin, Hatibagan Nabinpally Sarbojanin Durgotsav, from here take a bus toward Hedua and reach Kashi bose lane and Azad Hind Bag (Hedua Park). The next is “Simla Byayam Samity”. To reach here, come to Girish Park and take the metro, after taking a bus to Sovabazar. Visit Sovabajar rajbari puja. After taking an auto from there, reach Ahiritola Sarbojanin Durgotsav and Sovabazar Beniatola Durga puja. Later, take an auto or walk to Kumartuli Park Sarbojanin & Kumartuli Sarbojanin Durgotsab, Sovabazar Beniatola Durga puja and B K Paul. Next move for Jagat Mukherjee Park and reach Sovabazar. After moving to Bagbazar Sarbajanin, it is a 10-minute walking distance from Jagat Mukherjee Park. Next, take an auto to Bagbazar crossing and go to Syam Bazar and Deshbandhu Park. From Syambazar, take another bus for Tala Barowari and Tala Pratyay puja. In the meantime, you will get a lot of pujas on the roadside/ home/ alley, which you can enjoy also.
