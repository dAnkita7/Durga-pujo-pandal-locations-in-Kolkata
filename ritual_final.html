Durga Puja is a major Hindu festival celebrated primarily in West Bengal, India, and among Bengali communities around the world. It commemorates the victory of the goddess Durga over the buffalo demon Mahishasura. This fastival mainly celebrates the victory of good over evil. The festival typically lasts for ten days but majorly ten days and is marked by elaborate decorations, cultural events,processions, and rituals.
